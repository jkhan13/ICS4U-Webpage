ABOUT UNDERCOVER

October 27th, 2024

This website was created with the idea of a magazine, with a sleek design and satisfying hover effects(!!) but for the things that matter to me.

I feel like I've been so busy lately, as if school is my only life. Sure, it's, honestly, pretty fulfilling, but only up until a certain point. After that point in time, I almost forget what it's like to hang out with friends outside of school, I forget anything but that stressful go-go-go environment.

For me, it's experiences and people, outside of school that really make my life colourful, that fulfill me and help me feel my best. I designed this magazine with that in mind. The idea behind it is that sure, you may see me at school, this random girl with headphones that may as well be glued to her ears, and you'll think that's that. It's clich√©, but there's truly so much more. Even to me, it feels like I'm living a whole secret life when I leave that building, full of laughter and a little bit of healthy carelessness.

Living those moments, and then returning to school the next day, feels really strange, honestly.

It's almost as if I'm Undercover.

Please enjoy!
