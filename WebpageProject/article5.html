LDH KID INFILTRATES EOM SENIOR SUNSET

September 13th, 2024

With being a senior comes tons and tons of senior-related events, one of them being the Senior Sunset.

Honestly, I personally don't often participate in these events, because I'm usually busy with dance, school, or whatnot. But this time around, I was free. And, really, it's my last year of high school, wouldn't it be a waste to not participate in all the senior events?

Now the problem: who to go with. I asked around a bit, but any answers I got were vague, and no one knew if they were coming for sure. So, what did I do? I texted Niki (the aforementioned LDH kid). Surprise surprise. And she said yes!

And so, the day arrived. We met up at Kanata Centrum's Coco for some bubble tea and, naturally, to look at the stationary, of course. I suggested walking over, and we headed off.

We eventually made it with our limbs intact (and only having spilled a little bit of bubble tea on my white skirt), and we just walked around, talking to different people from my school. We had a lot of fun, honestly, and the pictures turned out amazing! Everyone kept on referring to her as the “Famous Niki”, though… Do I really talk about her that much…

The only downside: mosquitoes. Who decided to host this on a field…

After an hour or so, we had already suffered enough mosquito bites, and it was getting dark, so we got picked up by my mom.

We got to my place, and after scouring our medicine cupboards, I eventually found some Claritin, and oh boy, was it a blessing.

At the end of the day, though, despite the mosquitoes, we both had a lot of fun. I'm really happy I went and got Niki to come along, too, because I really do want to get the full senior experience of high school, and I'm glad I get to do it with a friend.
